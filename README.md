## Profile Summary
I am a junior at **Manchester Metropolitan University** focused on large language models and multimodal systems. My work spans the full model lifecycle: instruction data engineering, supervised fine-tuning, alignment optimization, RLHF strategy design, reinforcement learning experiments, evaluation architecture, and serving-oriented optimization.

I focus on building models that perform well not only on offline benchmarks, but also under practical product constraints such as latency, stability, controllability, and cost-efficiency.

## Core Focus
| Direction | Scope |
|---|---|
| **LLM Engineering** | End-to-end post-training pipelines from data curation to deployment-ready checkpoints. |
| **SFT Fine-Tuning** | Instruction formatting, data quality control, hyperparameter strategy, and repeatable training workflows. |
| **RLHF + Reinforcement Learning** | Preference data construction, reward-oriented optimization, and alignment behavior improvement. |
| **Multimodal Models** | Text-image data pipelines, multimodal instruction tuning, evaluation, and robustness analysis. |
