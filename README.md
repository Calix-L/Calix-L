## About Me
I am a junior at **Hubei University** focused on large language models and multimodal systems. My work spans the full model lifecycle: instruction data engineering, supervised fine-tuning, alignment optimization, evaluation design, and serving-oriented optimization.

I focus on building models that perform well not only on offline benchmarks, but also under practical product constraints such as latency, stability, and controllability.
