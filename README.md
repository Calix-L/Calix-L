I am a junior at **Hubei University** focused on large language models and multimodal AI systems. I work across the full lifecycle of model development: data curation, supervised fine-tuning, alignment optimization, evaluation, and production-oriented inference.

I am especially interested in building LLM systems that are not only strong on benchmarks, but also reliable and useful in real-world applications.
